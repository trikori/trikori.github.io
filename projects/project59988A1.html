    • Tonal Wave

    For this project, I wanted to experience music with physical feeling, being a part of music itself. We all love music, it provokes us, gives us a feeling of connectivity, a whole world of experience. Thus, we have Tonal Wave, a musical device where users embody the music they play.

    There is a distance sensor placed apart from the main box, which is use with the hand. The main box contains a button, a trimpot, and two force sensitive resistors. Those are placed in a comfortable position. The sounds are design in Pure Data.
