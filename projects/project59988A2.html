    • Calware

    For this project, I wanted to visually see how much calories we consume. Majority of us do not actually know how much calories we consume each day for each meal. Thus, we have Calware, a way to physically visualize our personal calorie consumption, which could provide us a better insight into our diet.

    A motor is used to provide motion for the conveyor belt. The main structure is a cardboard automata. There are two cardboard cylinders at each end to create a moving mechanism for the cardboard conveyor belt. A cardboard wheel is used to help create motion to the cylinders. The face mask represents a human face consuming calories balls. The calories balls are styrofoam balls.
