    • Hope & Beauty

    For this project, I wanted to reprsent what Spring and reawakening would look and feel like through the lifecycle of as a butterfly!

    Two servos were used to create the wings' movements. A flex sensor was used to create the opening of a cocoon to represent the Spring and Reawakening concept.
